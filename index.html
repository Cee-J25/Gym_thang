Plan Your Routine

Split your week into workout sections—like cardio one day, strength the next. This keeps things balanced and prevents burnout.

Be Flexible

Listen to your body. If something isn’t working, adjust. Fitness is a journey, not a race.

Know Your Body

Your weight and goals matter. Want to lose fat? Focus on cardio. Trying to gain muscle? Lift more and eat more.

Eat Smart

Fuel up with protein, healthy fats, and carbs. Stay hydrated. Your diet should support your fitness goals.

Rest is Key

Don’t overtrain. Take rest days to let muscles heal and grow. Recovery matters as much as workouts.

Track Progress

Write down workouts, reps, and how you feel. It keeps you focused and shows how far you've come.
